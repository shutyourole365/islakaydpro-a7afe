# Data Retention Policy

**Effective Date:** February 7, 2026

This Data Retention Policy outlines how Islakayd ("we," "us," or "our") collects, stores, and deletes user data on our equipment rental marketplace platform (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

## 1. Types of Data and Retention Periods

### 1.1 Account Data
- **Personal Information**: Name, email, phone, address - Retained for the duration of your account plus 3 years after account deletion for legal and tax purposes
- **Profile Data**: Photos, bio, verification documents - Same as above
- **Login History**: IP addresses, device info - 1 year for security purposes

### 1.2 Transaction Data
- **Booking Records**: Details of rentals, payments, communications - 7 years for financial compliance
- **Payment Information**: Card details are not stored; processed through secure third parties
- **Refund Records**: 7 years

### 1.3 Usage Data
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics data (if enabled) - 26 months as per Google's policy
- **Log Files**: Server logs - 90 days
- **Cookies**: As described in our Cookie Policy

### 1.4 Communication Data
- **Messages**: Chat and email communications - 3 years
- **Support Tickets**: 5 years

### 1.5 Content Data
- **Equipment Listings**: Retained until account deletion or equipment removal
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Retained indefinitely unless requested for deletion

## 2. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit
- Access is limited to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security audits and updates are performed

## 3. Data Deletion

### 3.1 User-Initiated Deletion
- Upon account deletion, most data is removed within 30 days
- Some data may be retained longer for legal reasons
- You can request complete data erasure under GDPR rights

### 3.2 Automatic Deletion
- Inactive accounts (no login for 2 years) may be flagged for deletion
- Temporary data (caches, sessions) is deleted regularly

## 4. Legal Retention Requirements

We retain certain data longer when required by law:
- Tax records: 7 years
- Financial transactions: 7 years
- Legal disputes: Until resolution plus 1 year

## 5. Data Minimization

We collect only the data necessary for providing the Service and comply with data minimization principles.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to servers in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses.

## 7. User Rights

Under applicable laws, you have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data (subject to legal requirements)
- Data portability
- Object to processing

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Retention Policy to reflect changes in laws or practices. Users will be notified of significant changes.
