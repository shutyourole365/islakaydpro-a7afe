# DMCA Policy

**Effective Date:** February 7, 2026

Islakayd ("we," "us," or "our") respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. This Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA") Policy outlines our procedures for responding to claims of copyright infringement on our equipment rental marketplace platform (the "Service").

## 1. Copyright Infringement Notification

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been infringed upon our Service, please send a written notification to our Designated Agent providing the following information:

### Required Information:
- A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative
- Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
- Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing and its location on the Service
- Your contact information, including address, telephone number, and email
- A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner
- A statement that the information in the notification is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

## 3. Counter-Notification

If you believe that your content was removed or access was disabled by mistake, you may file a counter-notification with our Designated Agent. Your counter-notification must include:

- Your physical or electronic signature
- Identification of the material that has been removed and its location before removal
- A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed by mistake
- Your name, address, and phone number
- A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district

## 4. Repeat Infringers

We have a policy of terminating the accounts of users who are repeat copyright infringers in appropriate circumstances.

## 5. False Claims

Please note that under Section 512(f) of the DMCA, any person who knowingly materially misrepresents that material is infringing may be subject to liability for damages.

## 6. Process Timeline

- Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing material within a reasonable time
- We will notify the user who posted the content
- If a counter-notification is received, we may restore the content after 10-14 business days, unless the original complainant initiates legal action
